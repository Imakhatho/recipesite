Recipe

Chocolate Cake

"Something Sweet, something soft, something tasty!! did you say... chocolate cake?"

Difficulty Prep time Cooking Total time
Easy:4/10 12 mins 25 mins 37 mins

Ingredients:

Flour : 100 g

Eggs : 6

Butter : 300 g

Sugar : 200 g

Milk : 1 liter

Salt : 4 g

Baking soda : 30 g

Chocolate : 400 g

Preparing:

1

Prepare Baking Pans

2

Allow ingredients to reach room Temp

3

Preheat oven

4

Stir together dry ingredients

5

Combine the Butter and sugar

6

Add eggs one at a time

7

Alternate, adding dry and wet ingredients

8

Pour batter into pans and bake
